Robot Fight Club Logo

Robot Fight Club is a fast-paced game of kids fighting with robots in an abandoned futuristic academy.

The logo

The logo is meant to represent the sign of the former Robot Club, found in the abandoned academy, “fixed” by the kids to say “Robot Fight Club” instead. The sign has embossed letters and is very worn and rusty along the edges. The “FIGHT” is made to be painted on by the kids.
